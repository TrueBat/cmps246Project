This website was created in the 8th of april 2022, to make game browsing easier and more accessible to everyone. We're simply two modest web developers trying to have fun while also attempting to make it easier for those who want to find games they enjoy, rather than just browsing aimlessly on the internet. This website has a system that stores games and organizes them into categories so that you can discover your favorite genre easily. The genres range from puzzle games and brain teasers, to high action first person shooters, and even story-driven single player games.
